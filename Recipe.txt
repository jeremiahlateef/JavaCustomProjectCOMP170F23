Recipe Name- Aloo Tikki
Allergens- None
Preference- Vegan
Taken from- https://hebbarskitchen.com/aloo-tikki-recipe-aloo-ki-tikki/#Ingredients_1x2x3x
Ingredients-
3 potato / aloo, boiled & grated
1 chilli, finely chopped
1 tsp ginger garlic paste
¼ tsp turmeric
½ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
½ tsp cumin powder / jeera powder
½ tsp aamchur / dry mango powder
½ tsp chaat masala
½ tsp salt
2 tbsp mint / pudina, chopped
2 tbsp coriander, chopped
2 tbsp corn flour
oil, for frying

Method-

Step 1- In a large mixing bowl take 3 boiled and grated potatoes. make sure to pressure cook potatoes for 12 minutes and drain off the water as soon as pressure releases.
Step 2- Add 1 chilli, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, ¼ tsp turmeric and ½ tsp chilli powder.
Step 3- Also add ½ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp aamchur, ½ tsp chaat masala and ½ tsp salt, and also add 2 tbsp mint and 2 tbsp coriander.
Step 4- Now add 2 tbsp cornflour and mix well alternatively use poha powder or rice flour as it helps to absorb moisture. Mix well forming a soft dough.
Grease hand with oil and prepare ball-sized tikki.
Step 5- Shallow fry it in hot oil. you can alternatively roast or deep fry. If roasting, roast on low flame until it turns golden brown. Flip over and cook on both sides.
Step 6- Finally, enjoy aloo tikki with green chutney, tamarind chutney.
Note: You can purchase the powders, spices mentioned in the recipe from Indian or Asian grocery stores.
